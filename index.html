About Me...

Hello!, I'm Cade, A Front-end developer & UI/UX designer from the UK.

I have recently finished working 2 years for a company as a UI/UX Designer and Developer. Just before that, I had graduated in my Level 3 Extended Course in BTEC IT with a DDD. I have always had a passion and never ending interest for technology. I’m a huge believer in the idea that when you work on something you have a passion for or enjoy, you will do a much better job rather than working on something you have no interest in.

I continuously work on my skill set and broaden my knowledge in my area of expertise. I‘ve had many happy clients who I have done work for.
